3. The Google Business Profile Audit

Your Google Business Profile (GBP) is your new storefront. Here is exactly how to optimize it for high-ticket clients:

  • Name & Category: Ensure your primary category is exactly what makes you the most money (e.g., "Car detailing service"). Do not keyword stuff.
  • High-Ticket Services: List out PPF, Ceramic Coating, and Paint Correction as individual services with starting prices.
  • UGC & Reflections: Upload weekly videos. Post close-up videos of water beading on a fresh ceramic coating or mirror-like reflections.
