3. The Ultimate Google Business Profile Audit

Your Google Business Profile (GBP) is your new storefront. Here is exactly how to optimize it for high-ticket clients:

  • Name & Category: Ensure your primary category is exactly what makes you the most money (e.g., "Car detailing service" or "Auto window tinting service"). Do not keyword stuff your business name.
  • High-Ticket Services Setup: List out PPF, Ceramic Coating, and Paint Correction as individual services with robust descriptions and starting prices.
  • UGC & Reflections: Upload weekly User-Generated Content (UGC). Do not just post cars; post close-up videos of water beading on a fresh ceramic coating or the mirror-like reflection of a completed paint correction.
  • The "Keyword" Review Strategy: Train your staff to ask customers to mention specific services in their Google reviews (e.g., "Ask them to say: 'Paras did a great job on the Xpel PPF for my Tesla.'").
